For publishers

Wholesale leather bookbinding, edition by edition

Leather covers and presentation boxes for collected works, special editions and facsimiles, produced for publishers since 1978 and shipped worldwide.

GÜNALP is a second-generation Istanbul bindery working exclusively with publishers and institutions. If your list carries editions meant to last (collected works, anniversary printings, collector's editions), we produce the covers and boxes to match, wholesale and to programme.

Your press prints the book block; we craft the cover. Casing-in, mounting the cover onto the printed block, remains with your printer or binder. Registration, colour and delivery are held to the tolerances a publisher's schedule demands. For Qur'an and mushaf editions, see our Qur'an leather binding page.

Cover types

Three constructions, one standard

01Genuine leather

Full calfskin covers with deep embossing, hot foil and art-gilt edges, for flagship and collector's editions.

02Thermo leather

The economical workhorse for large religious and classical series, with the same dies and the same register.

03Quarter leather

A genuine-leather spine with bookcloth sides, the classical balance of durability and cost for collected works.

In series

One standard across the whole run

Graduated formats, multi-volume sets and repeat printings leave our benches to a single registration and colour standard; the last copy of the run matches the first. So do the slipcases, traycases and book-form boxes that carry them.

Every copy is checked by eye before it is packed.

Facsimile editions

Covers and boxes for facsimile publishing

For facsimile publishers we reproduce classical bindings in their original vocabulary: blind-embossed grounds, multi-tone hot foil laid in exact register, shamsa and border compositions.

Our work in this field includes production for the Türkiye Manuscripts Institution (Yazma Eserler Kurumu).
