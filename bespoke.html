Book-form box

Crime & Punishment presentation box

A box that reads as a book. Leather-covered on a rigid structure, hot-foil title and rule-work on the spine and boards, opening to a custom-fit interior.

The laser-cut interior is shaped to hold its contents exactly — no movement, no filler. The same object is produced in more than one colourway to match a series or a gift programme.
