Why GÜNALP

Hand-operated presses, kept that way by choice

No motor drives our presses, a craftsman's hand does. Reading the pattern as he works, he eases the leather into relief as deep as 2 mm: a depth no fully automated line has ever reached.

And Istanbul lends the work one more quiet advantage: the same handwork, the same patience, at rates that sit well below Western European binderies.
